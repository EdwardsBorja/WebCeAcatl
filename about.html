About me

I am a Computer Systems Engineering student, currently in the penultimate semester of my degree. I specialize in web development and database management, with hands-on experience in projects that combine appealing design, functionality, and accessibility. I work with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind, Firebase, and MySQL, as well as development tools like Apache NetBeans and Linux environments (Ubuntu).

I have worked on both academic and personal projects, including web applications, database management systems, and innovative solutions for everyday problems. My focus is on building applications that are not only visually appealing but also efficient and user-friendly, with a strong emphasis on user experience and accessibility.

I am passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and create a positive impact in my community. I also have skills in video and audio editing, graphic design, web animations, and server management. I consider myself a self-taught, proactive, and detail-oriented person with a strong interest in growing professionally in the field of development and technological innovation.
